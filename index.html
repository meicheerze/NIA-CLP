FAQs

Could I able to train myself on all the four skills by being at home using the Online materials ?

  • Yes, You could definitely do it, but at the end you have to attach yourself with a consultancy and appear for the exams. Instead of approaching the consultancy at the end, if you link yourself earlier, you could get more materials for your practise and tips and guidance for your learning the course.

How many times in a year do they conduct BEC exams?

  • BEC is held on various fixed dates in centres worldwide. The tests are available monthly. Candidates must enter through an authorised centre.

Could I enroll myself for Preliminary in June and immediately for Vantage or Higher during the next exam in the month of August or September?

  • You need to wait till the result is published. Further, you need to have preparation time before you take up a level.

What is the level that I have achieved in IELTS if I passed my BEC Vantage with 182 marks?

  • If your BEC score is 182 then the IELTS equivalent score is between 6.5 to 7.0 band.

Is it good for me to provide Listening Skills through listening to various New’s channels and Hollywood Cinemas would improve my listening skills.

  • Yes, You could definitely get a good acquisition to listening skills. But as BEC is a Business English Certification, gaining listening practise should be in the area of Business oriented presentations.
